# Referral Message Templates

Plain text only. Personalize every send. Keep under 300 words.

---

## Template A: Warm 1st-Degree Connection

```
Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well. I saw [Company] is hiring for a [Role] role and it looks like a strong fit for my background in [your area].

I've spent [X years] doing [relevant work], most recently [brief current/recent role highlight]. The team's work on [specific product/initiative from public info] is especially interesting to me.

Would you be open to referring me, or pointing me to the right person on the hiring team? Happy to send my resume and a short summary of why I'm a fit.

Thanks either way — really appreciate it.

[Your name]
```

---

## Template B: Recruiter / TA Contact

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed the [Role] opening at [Company] ([job URL]) and wanted to reach out directly. My background is in [area] with experience in [2–3 relevant skills from job post].

I'm particularly interested because [one specific reason tied to company or role].

If you're the right contact, I'd love to be considered. If not, could you point me to whoever owns this req?

Thank you,
[Your name]
```

---

## Template C: Hiring Manager (Same Department)

```
Hi [Name],

I've followed [Company]'s work on [product/space] for a while. The [Role] position caught my attention because it lines up with what I've been building: [one sentence proof point].

At [Previous company], I [specific achievement with metric if possible]. I think that maps well to [requirement from job post].

No pressure at all — if you think there could be a fit, I'd welcome a chance to chat or be referred into the process.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

## Template D: Alumni / Peer (Weaker Tie)

```
Hi [Name],

[Mutual context: school, former company, event, group post].

I'm exploring a move into [target space] and saw the [Role] role at [Company]. Given you're there, I wanted to ask if referrals are something you're comfortable with for strong fits.

I can share a one-pager on my background if helpful. Totally understand if it's not the right time.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

---

## Template E: Follow-up (7 days, no reply)

```
Hi [Name],

Just bumping my note about the [Role] role at [Company] — still very interested if referrals are possible on your end.

If now's not a good time, no worries at all.

[Your name]
```

---

## Template F: Thank You After Referral

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you again for referring me for the [Role] role — I really appreciate you putting your name behind me.

I submitted my application and will keep you posted if anything moves forward.

[Your name]
```

---

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, verify:

- [ ] Correct role title and company name
- [ ] Job URL included (for recruiter template)
- [ ] One specific detail about them or the company (not generic)
- [ ] No markdown formatting
- [ ] Under 300 words
- [ ] Clear ask without being pushy
- [ ] User reviewed and approved final text

---

## Anti-patterns (Do Not Use)

- "I hope this message finds you well" (overused)
- "I'm a rockstar/ninja guru" (cringe)
- Mass-identical messages to 20 people same day
- Attaching resume without asking
- Pitching your startup unrelated to the job
- Name-dropping connections who didn't agree
